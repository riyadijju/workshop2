Thomas John Watson Sr.

Thomas J. Watson was an American businessman and the first CEO of International Business Machines (IBM). Watson was born on February 17, 1874 in Campbell, New York, the only son of Thomas and Jane Fulton White Watson. In his early twenties, Thomas had worked as a teacher, book keeper, butcher and piano peddler. Later in the year 1896, the young watson got employed as a cash register salesman in National Cash Register Company in Buffalo. He eventually got promoted in the NCR to work as a general sales manager which is when Mr. Watson initiated the motto "THINK", that later became the badge of IBM.
